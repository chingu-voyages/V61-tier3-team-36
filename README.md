# Overview

SpecForge is an AI-powered platform that helps transform a simple product idea into a detailed software specification.

Instead of starting with a blank document, users have a natural conversation with an AI assistant. The AI asks thoughtful follow-up questions to understand the product, its goals, users, features, and requirements. Once enough information has been gathered, SpecForge automatically generates a structured specification that can be used by developers, designers, product managers, or stakeholders.

The goal is to reduce the time spent writing requirement documents while ensuring that important details are not overlooked.

---

# Why SpecForge?

Many great ideas never become successful products because requirements are incomplete, unclear, or constantly changing. Teams often spend hours discussing features before they can even begin development.

SpecForge simplifies this process by guiding users through an intelligent interview that gradually uncovers every important aspect of the product.

Whether you're building a startup, a personal project, or an enterprise application, SpecForge helps organize ideas into a clear development plan.

---

# How It Works

### 1. Create a Workspace

Start by creating your own workspace where all your projects are securely stored.

---

### 2. Create a Project

Give your project a name and begin describing your idea.

---

### 3. Talk with the AI

Instead of filling out long forms, simply answer the AI's questions.

The AI adapts the conversation based on your responses, asking only the questions needed to fully understand your product.

---

### 4. Build the Specification

As the conversation progresses, the AI continuously gathers information about:

- Your product
- Target users
- Core features
- Functional requirements
- Business goals
- Possible edge cases
- Future improvements

---

### 5. Generate the Final Specification

Once enough information has been collected, SpecForge generates a complete software specification that is ready for review, sharing, or further development.
